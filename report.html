Understanding Cybercrime

Cybercrime refers to illegal activities carried out using digital devices or the internet. Examples include hacking, phishing, identity theft, and cyberbullying. It's important to report such crimes promptly to protect yourself and others.

Reporting Rules

  • Ensure you have all the evidence (e.g., emails, screenshots).
  • Do not confront the perpetrator directly.
  • Provide accurate information when filing the report.
  • Use secure channels to report sensitive data.

Legal Aspects

Cybercrime is punishable under the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other legal frameworks in India. Reporting such crimes helps law enforcement agencies take appropriate actions and prevents future incidents.
